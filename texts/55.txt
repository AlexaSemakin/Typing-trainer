She clasped little Gerda round the waist, and said, — "They shall not kill you as long as you don’t make us vexed with you. I suppose you are a princess. " "No," said Gerda; and then she told her all her history, and how fond she was of little Kay. The robber - girl looked earnestly at her, nodded her head slightly, and said, "They sha’nt kill you, even if I do get angry with you; for I will do it myself. " And then she wiped Gerda’s eyes, and stuck her own hands in the beautiful muff which was so soft and warm. The coach stopped in the courtyard of a robber’s castle, the walls of which were cracked from top to bottom.
